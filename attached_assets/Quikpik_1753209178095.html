How it works

STEP 1

Online stores install our plugin or Open APIIf needed, Quikpik will also integrate with OMS' like Shipstation

STEP 2

Your customer shops online and chooses pickup at checkout instead of opting for home delivery.

STEP 3

Your customer receives a confirmation email--it's time to pick up their parcel!- Email contains a unique pickup code
- Email generally received within a day of placing order

STEP 4

Your customer picks up the order- Pickup locations are local convenience stores
- Shows the shopkeeper their unique barcode to pick up the item. No need for ID.
